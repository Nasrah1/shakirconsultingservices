ABOUT US

Shakir Consulting Services was founded upon integrity, values, servitude, cultural responsiveness, and racial equity. Our vision is to be a leading consulting and social services agency owned by a Muslim American woman who is a descendant of slavery, that provides seamless and transformative services for those who are often overlooked or go unnoticed.

Our Consulting Services is a small social services and consulting agency that currently provides the following culturally specific services: Contracted case management services for Ramsey County's elderly and disabled, Culturally centered housing services through Housing Stabilization Services, Parent Support Outreach Services (PSOP) for residents of Ramsey County, Business Consulting, Racial Equity Training, Organization Development and Change Management.


Culturally Centered Housing Stabilization Services

Housing Stabilization Services is a Medical Assistance benefit to help people with disabilities and seniors find and keep housing. Housing stabilization services are state plan (not waiver) Home and Community-Based Services (HCBS).

Parent Support Outreach Program

Shakir Consulting is a new PSOP provider with Ramsey County. Ramsey County is committed to supporting healthy families and strong communities. Our Parent Support Outreach Program (PSOP) can help connect you to the services and resources your family needs, and work with you on support options to help your family thrive.

Culturally Specific Case Management

Shakir Consulting provides the following case management services to people of all ages and backgrounds in Ramsey County: Alternative Care (AC) Program Elderly Waiver (EW) Community Access for Disability Inclusion (CADI) Waiver Developmental Disability (DD) Waiver DD Non- Waiver Brain Injury (BI) Waiver Community Alternative Care (CAC) Waiver.

OUR AMAZING TEAM

Ayana Shakir, MA

President and CEO

Ayana is a Doctoral Candidate in Organization Development and Change Management at the University of St. Thomas. Ms. Shakir has a Master's of Arts in Health and Human Services Administration from St. Mary's University. She has over 20 years of experience as a Social Work Practitioner and Educator working in the areas of Adoption, Foster Care, Child Protection, Adult Protection, MN Choices DD Assessments, and taught English in Saudi Arabia at Taif University.

Hana Song, MSW, LGSW

Supervisor

Hana is the supervisor for the Alternative Care, Elderly Waiver, and CADI Waiver programs with Ramsey County. Ms. Song has over 12 years of experience in the aging and disabilities field.

Imad Mohamed, MSW

Program Coordinator

Imad experience includes emergency mental services with Hennepin County, child protection services with Hennepin County, and behavioral therapy with Minnesota Autism Center. Imad is also involved in the Equity Committee as an Equity Champion at DHS.

Hodan Ibrahim

Case Aide

Hodan has experience in Early Childhood Education, is bi-lingual in English and Somali, and knowledgeable about the Somali and Muslim communities in the Twin Cities. In addition, Ms. Ibrahim is a trained Somali interpreter.
