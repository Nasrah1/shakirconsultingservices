Our Mission

Our mission is to serve as a catalyst for others to realize their gifts, talents, and human potential.

ABOUT US

Shakir Consulting Services is a small social services and consulting agency that currently provides contracted case management services for Ramsey County's elderly and disabled. We also will be providing culturally responsive housing services through Housing Stabilization Services beginning in December 2020. Shakir Consulting Services was founded upon integrity, values, servitude, cultural responsiveness, and racial equity. Our vision is to be a leading consulting and social services agency owned by a Muslim American woman who is a descendant of slavery, that provides seamless and transformative services for those who are often overlooked or go unnoticed.

OUR SERVICES

Housing Stabilization Services

Housing Stabilization Services is a Medical Assistance benefit to help people with disabilities and seniors find and keep housing. Housing stabilization services are state plan (not waiver) Home and Community-Based Services (HCBS).

Parent Support Outreach Program

The Parent Support Outreach Program (PSOP) is an early intervention program providing short-term voluntary support for at-risk children and families identified through screened out child maltreatment reports, community or self-referrals.

Case Management

We offer case management services for Seniors and people diagnosed with a disability. Our Case managers help find programs and resources for people to learn job skills, get a job, develop life skills and find housing. We only contract with Ramsey County.

OUR AMAZING TEAM

Ayana Shakir, MA

President and CEO
